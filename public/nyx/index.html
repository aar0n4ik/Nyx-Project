How it works

Two engines, one invisible experience

Nyx pairs Tether QVAC (a full on-device AI stack) with Solana (instant, cheap settlement). The AI thinks locally on your machine; the chain only shows up when value moves.

\uD83E\uDDE0

QVAC brain, on your device

A local LLM with RAG runs through QVAC\u2019s OpenAI-compatible server \u2014 no data leaves your machine. Offline, Nyx falls back to a deterministic model so verdicts never stop.

\u26A1

Solana at settlement

Positions settle in USD\u20AE against verified final scores. Solana Actions & Blinks let anyone stake straight from X or Discord \u2014 one tap, no onboarding.

\uD83D\uDCE1

Verifiable data mesh

Live match state comes from TxLINE\u2019s on-chain oracle. QVAC\u2019s P2P layer gossips snapshots device-to-device, so the truth survives even when servers don\u2019t.
